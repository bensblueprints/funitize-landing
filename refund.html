Refund Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2025

At Funitize, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy explains our approach to refunds and cancellations.

Quick Summary: We offer a 7-day free trial and a 30-day money-back guarantee after your first paid subscription charge. No questions asked.

1. Free Trial Period

All new users receive a 7-day free trial with full access to all features. During the trial:

  • You can cancel at any time before the trial ends
  • You will not be charged if you cancel during the trial
  • If you don't cancel, you'll be automatically charged $12 for an annual subscription

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied after your first payment, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee:

  • Request a refund within 30 days of your first charge
  • Full refund, no questions asked
  • Processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunded to your original payment method

Eligibility

The 30-day guarantee applies only to:

  • First-time purchases (first subscription payment)
  • Requests made within 30 days of the charge

3. After the 30-Day Period

After the initial 30-day guarantee period:

  • Annual subscriptions are generally non-refundable
  • Exceptions may be made on a case-by-case basis for technical issues or extenuating circumstances
  • Contact support@funitize.com to discuss your situation

4. Subscription Renewal

For subscription renewals:

  • Renewals are charged automatically on your anniversary date
  • Renewal charges are typically non-refundable
  • Cancel before your renewal date to avoid being charged
  • Your service remains active until the end of your billing period even if you cancel

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email us at refunds@funitize.com
  2. Include your store URL and reason for refund (optional)
  3. We'll process your request within 48 hours
  4. Refunds typically appear within 5-7 business days

6. Cancellation Process

To cancel your subscription without requesting a refund:

  1. Go to your Shopify admin panel
  2. Navigate to Apps > Manage apps
  3. Find Funitize and click "Uninstall"
  4. Your subscription will be canceled immediately
  5. Access continues until the end of your billing period

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Technical Issues

If you experienced technical problems that prevented you from using the Service:

  • Contact support first so we can resolve the issue
  • If unresolved, a refund may be granted at our discretion

7.2 Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice by mistake, we will refund the duplicate charge immediately.

7.3 Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact us immediately at billing@funitize.com with details.

8. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in these cases:

  • After the 30-day guarantee period (except special circumstances)
  • If the app was uninstalled immediately after purchase to avoid payment
  • For subscription renewals (unless you can demonstrate we failed to notify you)
  • If you violated our Terms of Service

9. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds or prorated refunds for mid-cycle cancellations. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your paid period.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through the Service.

11. Contact Us

Questions about refunds or cancellations?

Email: refunds@funitize.com
Response time: Within 24 hours

Remember: You have a 7-day free trial to test everything risk-free, plus a 30-day money-back guarantee after your first charge. We want you to be happy with Funitize!
